How do Towkays find utopiagroup.com.my?

The traffic source determines whether the homepage needs to convince strangers or reassure warm referrals. Pick the primary channel.

A

Word of Mouth / WhatsApp Referral

A friend or business contact says "eh you should check out Utopia, they did my system" and sends the link. The Towkay arrives already half-convinced — they just need proof it's legit and a price.

Implication: Homepage = credibility + "here's what your friend got" + easy WhatsApp CTA

B

Google Ads / Search

They Google "AI for business Malaysia" or "business software KL" and click an ad. They're comparing you against 3 other tabs. Cold traffic — you have 5 seconds to hook them.

Implication: Homepage = immediate hook + differentiation + social proof fast

C

Social Media (TikTok / Instagram / Facebook)

They see a video or post about AI business results, click the bio link. Curious but skeptical. Want to see real results, not marketing fluff.

Implication: Homepage = continue the story from the post + show real results + quick conversion

D

Mostly Referral, Some Ads

Today it's 70% word-of-mouth (your 35+ companies talk). You want to scale with ads/social but the primary channel is still "someone told me about you."

Implication: Design for referral first, but make sure cold traffic can self-serve too
