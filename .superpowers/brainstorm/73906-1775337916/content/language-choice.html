What language for the Towkay?

Your current site is fully English. But the Towkay audience you described might respond better to something else.

A

Full English

Keep it professional, positions Utopia as premium/international. Works for scaling operators and corporates too. But might feel distant to a Towkay in Rawang running a tomato farm.

B

Full BM (Bahasa Malaysia)

Maximum relatability for the Towkay. "Ini untuk orang macam saya." But limits reach to English-speaking operators and makes the brand feel local-only.

C

Mixed / Manglish (the way Towkays actually talk)

English structure with BM phrases where it matters. Like how a Towkay actually explains things to a friend: "Boss, this one can track your sales automatically lah. Tak payah manually key in dah."

This is how referrals work — if your page sounds like how their friend described it, trust transfers instantly.

D

English site + BM sub-landing pages

Main site stays English (professional brand). But the industry-specific pages (car rental, cleaning, trading) are in BM/mixed — because those are the pages Towkays land on from referral links.

"Send this link to your friend" works best when the link speaks their language.
