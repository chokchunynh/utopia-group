What IS Boss OS in practice?

When the Towkay opens Boss OS on Monday morning, what do they see? This determines how we show it in the 3-phone mockup.

A

A Dashboard (read-only intelligence)

The Towkay opens a web app and sees: today's sales, staff attendance, top products, cash flow status, AI-generated insights ("Your Tuesday sales dropped 23% vs last month — here's why"). They look, they learn, they act offline.

Think: a smarter version of checking your bank app balance

B

A Command Center (read + act)

Dashboard + actions: approve purchase orders, assign staff, reply to customer inquiries through AI, trigger marketing campaigns. The Towkay doesn't just see the data — they run the business from it.

Think: a cockpit for your entire business

C

WhatsApp-First (AI assistant they chat with)

The Towkay doesn't open an app. They WhatsApp the AI: "How much did we sell today?" "Who hasn't paid?" "Send reminder to customer X." The AI replies with data, charts, and takes action. Boss OS lives where the Towkay already lives.

Think: like having a smart PA on WhatsApp 24/7

D

Hybrid (Dashboard + WhatsApp)

A web dashboard for deep analysis + WhatsApp AI for quick questions and actions. The dashboard is the brain, WhatsApp is the mouth. Towkay uses WhatsApp 80% of the time, opens the dashboard when they need to dig deeper.

This is how DKING Boss.OS actually works — PWA dashboard + WhatsApp integration
