Key design decisions:

  • Headline is outcome, not product: "Your entire business. One screen. Every morning." — not "AI-powered business management platform"
  • 3 phones show 3 real industries with specific, believable data — the Towkay sees their kind of business
  • ROI proof above the fold: "Most clients save RM8,000-15,000/month" — money language before feature language
  • Two CTAs: WhatsApp (primary, warm leads) + "See It Working" scroll anchor (curious but not ready)
  • Trust bar: 35+ companies, 400+ staff — compressed, not shouting
  • Interactive: In the real build, the 3 phones will be tappable — click to switch industries
